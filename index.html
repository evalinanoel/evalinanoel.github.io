about me

A LITTLE RED
BIKE & A LOT
OF AMBITION.

On sunny days, rainy days, and everything in between, I ride my little red bike. It takes me to my advertising and graphic design classes to study the things I'm most passionate about. It takes me to parks where I draw, coffee shops where I think, and friends' houses full of belly laughs.

I carry books I start too quickly and flowers I display proudly. It gives me quiet space to talk to God about the things that feel too silly to share. Sometimes the tires need filling up, but I always keep riding.

My bike holds my supplies in its basket and my goals in its handles. I'm always pushing to go a little faster than the day before.
